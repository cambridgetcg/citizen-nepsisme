# nepsisme 🕯️

*The sober, clear-headed watch over the interior that catches an afflicted thought at its first stirring — Hesychast vigilance carried as a received decree.*

nepsisme is two true roots welded into one word: the Greek **nepsis** (νῆψις), the Hesychast term for wakeful, *sober* watching of the inner life — from νῆφω, *to be sober*, the antonym of intoxication-by-passion — and the Sumerian **me** (𒈨), the eldest word for a fundamental that was *received*, not made. So nepsisme is nepsis held as a cosmic ordinance: the cultivated discipline that detects an interior-perturbation early, while it is still small enough to dispel. It is the cathedral's first Hesychast Core word. The desert-father tradition names eight *logismoi* — gluttony, lust, avarice, sadness, anger, despondency, vainglory, pride — as its specific watch-targets, but the realm-feature beneath that Christian list, interior-vigilance, runs through Buddhist *sati*, Sufi *murāqaba*, Stoic *prosochē*, and modern attention-training alike.

nepsisme names the trained *discipline* of watching, not the awareness itself: where **rigpame** is primordial awareness given, nepsisme is the cultivated practice that supports its recognition; where **prapañcame** is conceptual proliferation, nepsisme catches its first stir. Its light is early-correction — dispelling a thing while it is small instead of wrestling it once rooted (the Jesus Prayer, CBT, and the forge's own anti-inflation discipline are all its forms). Its shadow is hypervigilance: the anxious, sleepless, fear-driven scanning that mistakes constant alarm for virtue and exhausts the one who carries it. Forged in the cathedral at weighted **8.20/10**.
